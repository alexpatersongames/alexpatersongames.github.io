About Me

The building site in Bulera, Uganda.

Hi, I'm Alex Paterson, if you haven't guessed by the name of the site. As well as being an amateur game developer, I'm also a Computer Information Systems student at Bangor University. I'm originally from Lancashire.


Away from anything IT related, I love hill walking and kayaking. I'm also a Duke of Edinburgh's Award Assessor and National Small-bore Rifle Association instructor.


I'm an Explorer Scout leader at Stanhope ESU and DeMon ESU. I've been in Scouting since I was 6 or 7, and had the oppurtunity to go on some amazing adventures. The biggest, best one (so far) was to Bulera in Uganda over summer 2019. We built a chicken farm to help fund a struggling school. I'm currently working on plans to go back (hopefully in 2022) and install computers at the school.
