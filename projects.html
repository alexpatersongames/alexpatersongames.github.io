Forest Canyon

Forest Canyon (or forest.wad) is a map I made for Doom 2. It's the first proper Doom map I made and is set in a forested canyon with a nukage processing plant in. The player's mission is to shut down the nukage purifier by shooting a couple of rockets into it.

Download Here!

To play the map, you need either Doom 2 installed (available here) or it's open source clone, Freedoom Phase 2 (available here). Forest Canyon (running on Freedoom) is available to play in your browser here, although it is very slow and doesn't work on mobile. I would reccomend downloading it and playing with Doom 2.

It is only a single map and replaces MAP01.
