Space Suit Sally

After a space mission goes wrong, astronaut Sally Stewart is trapped underground on the icy moon of Triton. The worst bit is that she's not alone...

A 2D Platformer / Shooter hybrid I created for the Miz Jam 1 game jam.

Best played with an Xbox One controller.
